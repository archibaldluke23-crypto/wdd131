flexbox is commonly used for Navigation bars.

Flexbox is designed for one-dimensional layouts, 
meaning it arranges items in either a row or a column.

To use Flexbox, set the parent element's display to flex.

By default, Flexbox places items in a horizontal row.

There is a flex container, and each element inside it 
is a flex item.

flex-direction: row; controls the direction of the items:
row: Items are placed the same as the text direction.
row-reverse: Items are placed opposite to the text direction. // when reversing the start and end are reversed as well
column: Items are placed top to bottom.
column-reverse: Items are placed bottom to top.

justify-content
Controls alignment along the main axis (left and right):
flex-start: Items align to the left side of the container.
flex-end: Items align to the right side of the container.
center: Items align at the center of the container.
space-between: Items display with equal spacing between them.
space-around: Items display with equal spacing around them.

align-items(used on all items)/ align-self(used on one item)
Controls alignment along the cross axis (up and down):
flex-start: Items align to the top of the container.
flex-end: Items align to the bottom of the container.
center: Items align at the vertical center of the container.
baseline: Items display at the baseline of the container.
stretch: Items are stretched to fit the container.

Align-content determines the spacing between lines, 
while align-items determines how the items as a whole 
are aligned within the container. When there is only 
one line, align-content has no effect.

flex-wrap
Allows items to move onto a new line if there isn't 
enough room.
nowrap: Every item is fit to a single line.
wrap: Items wrap around to additional lines.
wrap-reverse: Items wrap around to additional lines in reverse.

flex-flow
combining flex-wrap with flex-direction 
flex-flow: row/column wrap;

flex: 1 1 250px;
Allows items to grow and share available space. They will
grow equally.
flex: {growRate} {shrinkRate} {beginningSize}

order: 0;
sets the order of a specific item from left to right
within the flexbox


To spread items across the container
Use justify-content: space-between;

To Align items vertically
Use align-items.

Use gap to space them out as well.
